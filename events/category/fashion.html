Bio

A live exhibition of dark fashion works. 
10:00 p.m., Saturday, November 14, 2009 
319 Scholes Street, Brooklyn, NY, 11206 (L to Grand) 

Featuring Bethany Shorb, Sinner/Saint, Bird Ov Prey, B.S. Mercantile, VDK, Ryan Brogen, Dethlab, Mike Servito, Cowboy Mark, Death Domain, Black Swan
