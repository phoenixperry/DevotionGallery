INFINITE LIGHT LIMITED EDITION Video Premiere

Friday, October 7, 2011. 7-10PM
Please join us for the world premiere of a new dance media art work from Infinite Light.
Choreographer Brooke Broussard and video artist Benton-C Bainbridge are joined by composer Ben Bromley for a new dance, video and music art work based on footage created for Xi'an, China's 2011 International Horticultural Exposition. Bainbridge shot dancers Chelsea Retzloff, Kana Kimura, Ruby MacDougall, Hayley Jones and Brooke Broussard herself in a green screen studio in Shanghai and then transformed them with analog and digital FX to create cosmic collisions, tree spirits and blossoming flowers in the spirit of the Expo.
The video and choreography can be seen nightly in Xi'an through October 22, playing on 80 meter sprays of water and organic-shaped LED screens, both on land and underwater. The premiere of INFINITE LIGHT LIMITED EDITION gives audiences their first chance to see this work without traveling to Xi'an, condensed into a sensuous single channel mix and set to a new score by Ben Bromley of NewVillager.

319 Scholes and Devotion Gallery present: The Shift 

A three day benefit celebrating one year of curation in the 11206 Brooklyn zipcode
August 27th, 8pm through Sunday August 29th at 8pm

THE SHIFT is a birthday benefit for Devotion Gallery and 319 Scholes. It includes an exhibition, art auction with all pieces capped at $250, multiple performances, high calibre live music, and workshops focused on music technology. If you missed the last year at these two ground breaking, kick-ass exhibition spaces, or want a sneak peak at what is in store for 2011, THE SHIFT is not to be missed. 
 
PARTICIPATING ARTISTS CURATED BY DEVOTION:  SOFTlab, Dan Tesene, Marius Watz, Benton Bainbridge, Chris Jordan, Joshue Ott, Bethany Shorb, Sougwen Chung, Aaron Meyers, Jorden Haley, David Last, Phoenix Perry, Micah Frank,

PARTICIPATING ARTISTS CURATED BY 319 SCHOLES: Lindsy Howard, Michelle Mayer, Duncan Malaschock, Alexandra Gorczynski, Dave Linnenbank 

FRIDAY NIGHT LINE UP CURATED BY DEVOTION IN ORDER OF Appearance:  8pm - 4am | Silent Art Auction of select works starts at 8pm, Tū,  Zach Layton, Bora Yoon, Black Swan, Columboid, Sepalcure, Dethlab, Todd Pendu, Mike Dextro

SATURDAY NIGHT LINE UP CURATED BY DEVOTION AND 319 SCHOLES IN ORDER OF Appearance:  7pm until 4am | Sindy Butz, Ted Hayes, Ezekiel Honig, RJ Valeo, Igal Nassima & Taylan Sinan Yilmaz 

SUNDAY: 319 Scholes brings artists and audiences together with culinary creations. Food is served from 3-5pm.
SATURDAY AND SUNDAY WORKSHOPS ORGANIZED BY DEVOTION: Max Fuel / Max4Live, Ableton: 101, Ableton: Advanced Seminar, DIY Electroacoustics: Pickups and SuperCollider.  (note class admission not included in ticket price or vise versa)
 

$10 per night - $25 3 day pass - Free Saturday and Sunday during gallery hours (12pm - 4pm) 
LOCATION: 319 Scholes, Brooklyn, NY, 11206 (L to Morgan)
