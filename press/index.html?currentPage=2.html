Sloppy Journalism that I guess we were happy to get? 

Thursday, July 22, 2010 at 2:52 PM Share Article | Email Article

It's every galleries dream that their show get picked up by the NYTimes right? This is the second time this year the NYTimes was gracious enough to cover our little gallery. We are honored. The first article on the Unsound show was a great article. This one though makes me wonder about the state of journalism graduates in America. Not only did the writer not even spell the artists names correctly, but she failed to mention the name of artists she talks about. Basic fact checking is the starting point for any story. 

That said, I guess we were happy for the exposure, we just wished they would of sent a writer who could understand electronic and biological art vs see it as pretty "Christmas lights." She also misquoted everyone she spoke with.... unfortunate. 

http://cityroom.blogs.nytimes.com/2010/06/03/nocturnalist-taking-the-canvas-digital/
